YouTube

video hosting that provides users with video storage, delivery and display services. YouTube has become the most popular video hosting site and the second most visited site in the world. Users can upload, view, rate, comment, add to favorites and share videos without violating the rules and policies for using the service. In January 2012, the site reached 4 billion daily video views. The site features movies, music videos, trailers, news, educational programs, and amateur videos, including vlogs, let's play, slideshows, humorous videos, and more. The site has various music charts showing user preferences based on geographic location.
